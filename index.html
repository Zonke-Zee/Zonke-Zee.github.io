About

I am an african women born and raised in Alexandra Township. I completed my Bachelor of Science in Computer Science degree from EDUVOS formerly known as Pearson Institute of Higher Education in 2021. I am a self-motivated individual with a passion for coding and software development. I have experience in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, JAVA and C++. I am eager to learn new technologies and improve my skills. I am currently looking for opportunities to apply my knowledge and contribute to exciting projects. I have also gained a lot of team working and leadership skills playing rugby and being captain that I believe can be essential for the work environment.
